About

Prime Fitness Club is a renowned fitness club that was established in 1982. It offers a comprehensive range of fitness services and equipment to help individuals achieve their health and wellness goals.

Prime Fitness Club stands out due to its long-standing history since 1982 and its commitment to providing both basic and cutting-edge fitness equipment. It is also the flagship brand of Prime Fitness Club Ltd, a UN-based health and fitness services provider.

Prime Fitness Club offers a wide variety of fitness equipment, including both traditional and newly introduced options. You'll find everything from classic cardio machines to the latest in strength training and innovative fitness technology.

Prime Fitness Club Ltd is the parent company of Prime Fitness Club. It specializes in providing integrated health club management solutions. With a focus on staying fit and healthy, the company pioneers innovative approaches to meet the evolving fitness requirements of its members.

Joining Prime Fitness Club is easy! You can visit our facility in person to learn more about membership options, benefits, and pricing. Alternatively, you can visit our website or contact our customer service for more information on membership enrollment.
